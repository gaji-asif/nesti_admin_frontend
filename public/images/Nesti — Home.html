    What is Nesti?

    Nesti is a platform for parents that connects families and brings together essential services, meetups, and activities.

    Here you can find other parents, share everyday moments, and discover activities and services for families in your area.

    Our goal is to build a community where it is easy to ask questions, chat, and find company for life's small and big moments.
